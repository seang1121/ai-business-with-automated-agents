  - All buttons minimum 48x48px tap targets

  - One clear call to action per section
  - No carousels, no hover-only menus, no confusing animations
  - WCAG AA color contrast throughout

SEO Built In:
  - Title tag targets "pressure washing Long Island"
  - Meta description targets Nassau & Suffolk County searches
  - LocalBusiness JSON-LD structured data in <head>
  - All town names listed in Service Area section
  - Semantic HTML5 (header, main, section, article, footer)
  - Descriptive alt text on all images

PHASE 2 — AI AGENT ARCHITECTURE (FUTURE)
============================================================

Once the website is live and you're getting form submissions,
Phase 2 adds AI agents to help you run the business efficiently.

CORE PRINCIPLE — Human in the Loop (Non-Negotiable):
  Agents DRAFT messages and tasks.
  They NEVER send or act without your approval.
  Every agent action is logged for your review.
  Keeps the business personal. Protects your reputation.

Approval Flow:
  Agent drafts → sends YOU a notification (text or email)
  → You approve, edit, or reject → THEN it sends

Planned Agents (in priority order):

1. LEADS AGENT
   Trigger: New form submission arrives from Formspree
   Action:  Drafts a personalized follow-up message to the lead
   You:     Review draft → approve → it sends the message
   Benefit: Respond to leads within minutes, 24/7

2. SCHEDULING AGENT
   Trigger: Lead is ready to book
   Action:  Suggests available time slots based on your calendar
   You:     Confirm the time → customer is notified
   Benefit: No back-and-forth phone tag

3. REVIEWS AGENT
   Trigger: Job is marked complete
   Action:  Drafts a thank-you + Google review request for that customer
   You:     Approve per job → message goes out
   Benefit: Consistent review requests, personal tone

4. FINANCE AGENT
   Trigger: Job complete, price agreed
   Action:  Generates invoice with job details, sends for your review
   You:     Review → send to customer
   Benefit: Professional invoicing, no paperwork headaches

5. MARKETING AGENT
   Trigger: You complete a great job with good before/after photos
   Action:  Drafts social post captions for Facebook/Instagram/NextDoor
   You:     Approve → post yourself or auto-publish
   Benefit: Consistent social presence without the time drain

6. ESTIMATING AGENT
   Trigger: New lead with address and service type
   Action:  Generates a price estimate based on service + property size
   You:     Review and confirm before quoting the customer
   Benefit: Faster, more consistent quotes

Website Forms Are Agent-Ready:
  All form fields use clean, consistent names:
    name, phone, email, call_time, service, address, notes
  Formspree supports webhooks — pipe form data to an agent workflow
  No custom field names that would break automation later

WHY THESE DESIGN CHOICES WORK
============================================================

Research findings that drove the design:
  - 78% of local mobile searches convert within 24 hours
  - Before/after galleries are the #1 trust-building element
  - Response speed matters more than price to most homeowners
  - Elderly users have longer decision cycles — trust signals matter more
  - Mobile users tap phone numbers directly — make them obvious and big

What makes this site different:
  - Phone number in sticky header = always one tap away
  - Hero phone number is 4rem+ = impossible to miss
  - Dual CTA: Call Now (immediate) + Callback Form (non-phone users)
  - Service cards show real prices — builds trust, reduces friction
  - Town name list boosts Google rankings for local searches
  - No clutter, no confusion — one job per section
