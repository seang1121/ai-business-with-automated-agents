Meet Dr. James Chen

DDS, NYU College of Dentistry

Dr. Chen founded Bright Smile Dental with a simple belief: no one should dread going to the dentist. After graduating from NYU College of Dentistry and completing his residency at Mount Sinai, he spent over a decade perfecting techniques that minimize discomfort and maximize results.

He specializes in treating anxious patients and takes extra time to explain every procedure, answer questions, and ensure you feel in control throughout your visit. Whether it is your first cleaning in years or a complex restoration, Dr. Chen and our team will make sure you are at ease.
