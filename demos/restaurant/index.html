Our Story

A kitchen built on love and tradition

MR

Chef Marco Russo

Owner & Executive Chef

Marco is a third-generation Italian cook who grew up elbow-deep in flour at his grandmother Rosa's kitchen table in a small town outside Bologna. He spent his twenties training under master chefs across Emilia-Romagna, learning the art of sfoglia, slow-braised ragù, and the kind of hospitality that makes every guest feel like family.

In 2012, Marco opened Nonna's Kitchen in the heart of Westchester County to honor his grandmother and her belief that the best meals are the ones you share. Every dish on our menu traces back to a handwritten recipe card, stained with olive oil and love. Whether we're serving a Tuesday night dinner for two or catering a 200-guest wedding, the philosophy is the same: simple ingredients, proper technique, and generosity at the table.
