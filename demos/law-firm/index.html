Meet Your Attorney

SS

Sarah Smith, Esq.

Founding Partner & Lead Attorney

Sarah earned her Juris Doctor from Harvard Law School, where she focused on family and juvenile law. Before founding Smith & Associates, she spent three years as a clerk in Nassau County Family Court, giving her an insider's perspective on how judges evaluate cases and make decisions.

That courtroom experience shapes every strategy she builds today. Sarah understands that behind every filing is a real family navigating one of the most difficult chapters of their life. She combines rigorous legal preparation with genuine compassion to deliver outcomes her clients can build a future on.
